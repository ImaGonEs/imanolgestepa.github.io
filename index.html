Imanol G. Estepa

Hi! I'm a PhD student at the University of Barcelona supervised by Prof. Petia Radeva. My research focuses on the unification of successful representation learning approaches and generative modelling algorithms, so as to form rich latent spaces useful for both generative and discriminative tasks. Lately, I've been interested in generative Latent modification and interpretability.


Imanol G. Estepa is a PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Petia Radeva at the University of Barcelona. Throughout his studies, he has shown expertise in Self-supervised representation learning, collaborating on international projects and publishing his research at major computer vision and machine learning conferences. He completed a Computer Science bachelor's degree at the University of Deusto and moved to Barcelona to continue his studies with a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya. His current research focuses on Unified Self-supervised deep learning, bringing together Image Generation algorithms and modern Self-supervised approaches.
